The most delicious high-protein honey butter chicken alfredo pasta. All for 630 calories, so creamy and packed with flavor. Easy to make and perfect meal prep for the week. Slice your chicken into small cubes. Season with salt, chili flakes, parsley, garlic and onion powder, smoked paprika and olive oil. Mix till the color changes then cook the chicken on medium heat for 4 minutes each side until golden brown and crispy like this. Then low the heat, add more butter, drizzle of honey, soy sauce and fresh parsley. Give it a good mix till juicy and delicious then set the chicken aside. Add lots of garlic and sun-dried tomatoes. Cook this for 3 minutes before adding Italian herbs, salt, low-fat milk, light cream cheese with grated parmesan plus the rind. Keep stirring till completely smooth and slightly thickened then add your cooked pasta with fresh parsley. Give this a good mix till creamy and indulgent. Finally, your high-protein meals are ready to serve and enjoy for the week.
