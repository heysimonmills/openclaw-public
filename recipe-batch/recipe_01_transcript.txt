If you're looking for delicious, high-protein and low-calorie meals to lose weight, these orange chicken rice balls are incredibly delicious, only 540 calories and so easy to meal prep for the week. Slice your chicken thighs into cubes, season with black pepper, garlic and ginger paste, red chili flakes, honey, sriracha, fresh orange juice and zest, and soy sauce. If the colour doesn't change, it's not well seasoned. Next, add light butter on medium heat and toast your sesame seeds until slightly golden and crisp, then add minced garlic and cook for 2 minutes until fragrant before adding salt and leftover cooked rice. Give this a good mix till fluffy and well combined, then add chopped green onions and sesame seeds. Mix well, then slice all your veggies, then add rice vinegar, red pepper flakes and a drizzle of honey. Give this a good mix, then lay down your chicken thighs on a sheet pan and oven bake or air fry for 20 minutes until golden and juicy just like this.
