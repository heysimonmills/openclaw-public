This is your sign to stop eating boring and bland chicken and rice and instead make this crispy honey garlic chicken fried rice that's high in protein and perfect for meal prep. Add salt reduced soy sauce to your chicken with garlic, black pepper, onion powder then mix it well. Add it to a ziplock bag with some potato starch and mix it well until the chicken is fully coated. Then spray with light cooking oil and air fry with the settings on screen until golden and crispy. Now to a pan on medium heat add frozen mixed vegetables followed by some mixed eggs. Let it cook through and mix it well. Then add the white part of the spring onion followed by day old cooked white rice. Add a little bit of soy sauce, mix well then add the green part of the spring onion and mix well to combine. Pour the honey garlic sauce to a pan on medium heat add garlic, equal parts water and soy sauce, reduced sugar ketchup, rice vinegar, cornflour mixed with water to help it thicken then take it off the heat, add honey and mix once more. Now coat the chicken in the sauce, divide everything into 7 equal servings and enjoy.
