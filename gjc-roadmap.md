Throughout Phase 1, capture data to answer:

3. Which channels perform best? (Instagram vs TikTok vs ads)
4. What do customers love/want improved? (Feedback, reviews)
5. What's the repeat purchase rate? (Email engagement, retention)

These learnings feed directly into Phase 2 brand refresh decisions.
