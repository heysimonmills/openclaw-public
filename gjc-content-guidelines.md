## 📹 Filming & Production

### Setup Requirements
- **Always film in front of a window** for natural lighting
- Better lighting = better video quality and engagement

### Batching Workflow
Content creation follows a batched approach:
- **Day 1:** Ideation
- **Day 2:** Script writing
- **Day 3:** Filming
- **Day 4:** Editing
- **Then:** Schedule in advance
